**Mafia Bot - Privacy Policy**

Last updated: March 2026

**We collect no personal data.**

- The bot temporarily reads the list of users in a voice channel when `/playmafia` is used.
- It sends private DMs with role information.
- All data is discarded immediately after use.
- No logs of user activity are kept.

We do not share any information with third parties.

**Important:** Always get **explicit consent** from everyone in the voice channel before starting a game. The bot promotes playful social deduction, but consent is required.
